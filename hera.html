HERA

Hydropower and Environmental Resource Assessment

Download HERA

HERA is a software that supports the development of hydropower and pumped hydro storage projects. It studies the hydropower potential of river basins, considering project economic feasibility, as well as social and environmental impacts. HERA contributes to the stakeholder decision process, which is complex as it usually involves conflicting interests, pro and against the construction of dams, trying to balance energy production with conservation.

Use HERA for:

Construction simulation

Simulates actual construction of power plants, including reservoir flooded areas

Structure design

Using a database with different types of dams, spillways. powerhouses, canals, tunnels, etc.

Cost calculation

Considering electro-mechanical and socioenvironmental costs

HERA maximizes the economic benefit while minimizing the socioenvironmental impacts

The optimization process is formulated as a non-linear, integer, stochastic mathematical problem. Its objective is the selection of the hydropower and PHS projects to develop, considering also other benefits to the electrical grid, such as the integration of variable renewable energy sources.

Geoprocessing capabilities

In order to accurately simulate the construction of hydropwer and PHS projects, HERA uses the Digital Terrain Model, characterization of geological information and hydrology.

HERA computes the intersections between reservoir flooded area and layers of user provided information to determine costs related to environmental aspects and constraints. For each site, different options can be studied by varying the head and engineering layouts. A plant scheme of each project with estimated costs and installed capacity can be visualized.

  • Land cover
  • Population density
  • Environmental protected areas
  • Urban and rural areas
  • Pipelines, channels and others

Integrating renewable resources into power grids

Conventional hydro power and pumped hydro storage projects are essential for integrating Variable Renewable Energy (VRE) sources into power grids, as they significantly enhance grid flexibility. This is why HERA is designed to integrate seamlessly with the expansion planning tool OptGen and the dispatch planning tool SDDP. Hera generates hydro candidates that can be selected by OptGen and operated in detail by SDDP, providing a comprehensive solution for efficient energy management.
