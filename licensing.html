Licensing

Discover our software licensing models.

Perpetual License

A one-time payment grants perpetual access to the software, including M&S services for the first year. The latest software version released within that year can be run forever. After the initial year, new releases and M&S services are available via subscription.

Annual Subscription

The annual subscription provides access to the software, updates, and M&S services for one year. To maintain continuous access to the software, its new features and benefits, the subscription must be renewed for another year.

A flexible licensing model with unlimited installations

Our flexible licensing model offers great convenience and scalability. With unlimited installations on different machines, you can use our platform on any computer without the restriction of named licenses. Active licenses are only required to run models, allowing team members to analyze results through the GUI on their individual computers with ease.
