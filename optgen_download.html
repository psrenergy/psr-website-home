About OptGen 8.1

OptGen 8.1 introduces significant enhancements designed to strengthen long-term Generation and Transmission expansion planning studies. This version focuses on boosting computational efficiency and improving integration with complementary tools like SDDP and PSRCloud. Users will benefit from a more robust and capable system that handles complex planning scenarios with greater ease and precision.

Key improvements in this release include advancements in the data reading procedures, an optimized decomposition algorithm, and a more effective optimization solver. These refinements ensure that the software operates more smoothly and efficiently, providing users with a powerful toolset for their planning needs. Overall, OptGen 8.1 represents a substantial step forward in the software's development, delivering better performance and enhanced capabilities.
